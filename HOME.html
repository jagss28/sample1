THE D G RUPAREL COLLEGE

ARTS,COMMERCE AND SCIENCE

The D. G. Ruparel College, simply known as Ruparel College, was founded in 1952. The campus is located in Mahim, an inner suburb of Mumbai. It offers undergraduate and graduate degrees, including PhD programs in Botany, Chemistry, Physics, and Zoology. With an enrollment of around 1,500 students, the campus features professional landscaping, including a botanical garden.[4]
Ruparel College has been visited by several historical figures, including Indira Gandhi, Neelam Sanjiva Reddy (former President of India), P.C. Alexander (former governor of Maharashtra), and Ram Kapse (former Lt. Governor of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands).
